I am a PhD candidate in the German Department at Vanderbilt. During my undergraduate education, I spent two semesters in Würzburg, Germany, and after completing my BA, I spent two years as a Fulbright English Teaching Assistant in Austria. I have taught German at Davidson College, UC Berkeley, Belmont University, and Vanderbilt. I spent the 2015-16 year as an Ernst Mach Fellow at the University of Vienna doing research for my dissertation on the poetics of Austrian rap music. In the 2016-17 academic year, I was a Mellon Digital Humanities Fellow at Vanderbilt, and I am currently a Graduate Affiliate at the Center for Second Language Studies, where I am leading a working group on immersion and virtual reality in language classes.
